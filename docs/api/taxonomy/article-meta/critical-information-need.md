# Critical Information Need

Critical information need metadata (`meta_type=information_needs`) describes the practical civic or community information need a story helps readers meet. Articles may carry one to three critical information need tags.

## Filter

```text
?meta=information_needs:transportation
```

Discover values in your project with `information_needs_categories` from [Article facets](../../articles/facets.md) or `GET …/articles/metadata/types/information_needs/values`.

## Categories

| API value | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `emergencies_risks` | Information people need to prepare for, respond to, or recover from immediate or long-term risks |
| `health_welfare` | Physical health, mental health, public health, social services, care access, benefits, or basic well-being |
| `education` | Schools, colleges, educational access, school quality, student outcomes, education policy, or choices for families and students |
| `transportation` | How people move through the community, including systems, routes, costs, access, safety, and disruptions |
| `economic_opportunities` | Jobs, wages, business conditions, job training, entrepreneurship, household finances, or local economic opportunity |
| `environment` | Local environmental conditions, environmental risks, natural resources, climate impacts, restoration, or recreation access |
| `civic_information` | Community institutions, services, associations, participation, and shared civic life |
| `political_information` | Candidates, elected officials, campaigns, elections, voting, public policy, legislation, or government decisions |
| `other` | The article does not clearly serve one of the standard critical information needs |
