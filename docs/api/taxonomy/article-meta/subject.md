# Subject

Subject metadata (`meta_type=subject`) identifies the **concrete primary thing** a story is about — a specific incident, institution, project, case, or work — rather than a broad topic area. Articles typically carry **one** subject tag.

## Filter

```text
?meta=subject:development_project
```

Discover values in your project with `subject_categories` from [Article facets](../../articles/facets.md) or `GET …/articles/metadata/types/subject/values`.
